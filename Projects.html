Crochet Projects

There are many different types of crochet projects and they range from quick beginner friedly items to complex garments and blankets. People also commonly crochet unique designs and objects as room decor or just for fun!

Clothing Projects

Crochet projects can be quick, one day items like scrunchies, coasters, and chapsick holders to larger, long term endeavors like blankets adn garments. Some popular beginner friendly options include dishcloths, granny squares, scarves, bandanas, soap saver bags, keychains, laptop cases, headbands, bracelets, and beginner amiguruimi projects.

Home Decor Projects

Home decor crochet projects are a fun and depending on what you make, sometimes easy. They can range from funcional storage to decorative accents. Popular items include storage baskets, pillows, blankets, plant hangers, garlands, coasters, rugs, stuffed animals, and other creative and unique projects that add personalized style to spaces.

Amigurumi & Toys Projects

Amigurumi originates from Japan, with the term combining the Japanese words ami (crocheting/knitted) and nuigurumi (stuffed doll). Key techniques include using a "magic ring" as a start, increasing and decreasing for shaping, and stitches.
