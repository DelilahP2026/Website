Simple Stiches (and How to do Them)

Crochet stiches are made by using a crochet hook. There are many different sizes that vary based on what yarn size you need, or how much space you want between the stiches. The most used stiches include the chain (ch), slip stich (sl st), single crochet (sc), half double crochet (hdc), double crochet (dc), and treble (tr) stich. There are more complicated stiches such as the moss stich (linen stich), lemon peel stich, shell stich, popcorn stich, camel stich, and the crunch stich.
